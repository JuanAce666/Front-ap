What Do We Do?

What Do We Do?

Misión

Our mission at Mywellbeing is to facilitate the academic and professional success of STEM career students through a comprehensive platform that provides resources, support and guidance based on the best practices of the software life cycle, thus promoting their comprehensive development.

Visión

To be the leading tool that empowers students in STEM careers, providing them with an innovative web platform, personalized support resources and an evaluation system that allows them to effectively navigate their academic training and achieve their professional goals.
